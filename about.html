About Thought Glow

Thought Glow is an initiative of Lekh App. Lekh App is an intelligent whiteboarding and diagramming app.

Thought Glow is supported by Lernen. Lernen is an e-learning platform which provides various online courses such as Service Now Fundamental, Business Analyst etc.

Thought Glow's aim is to create an active and dynamic community of Business Analysts, Product Managers, Data Scientists and Programmers. The community can share knowledge with each other. We actively host online events to provide cutting edge information and provide guidance to individuals if they need.
